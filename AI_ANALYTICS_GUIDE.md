# MainPro AI Analytics & Auto-Suggestions Guide

## Overview
MainPro Calendar now features **AI-powered analytics and auto-suggestions** that provide intelligent insights, predictive maintenance recommendations, and compliance alerts to optimize your facility management operations.

- 🤖 **AI Analytics**: Real-time performance metrics and insights
- 🔮 **Predictive Maintenance**: AI-powered maintenance scheduling
- 🚨 **Compliance Alerts**: Automated compliance monitoring and alerts
- 💡 **Auto-Suggestions**: Smart task recommendations and workload optimization
- 📊 **Performance Dashboard**: Comprehensive analytics visualization
- ⚡ **Real-Time Updates**: Continuous analysis and recommendations

## AI Analytics Features

### 📊 Performance Metrics
**Task Completion Rate**
- Tracks percentage of tasks completed on time
- Calculated over the last 30 days
- Color-coded indicators (Green: >80%, Yellow: 60-80%, Red: <60%)

**Compliance Score**
- Monitors compliance task completion
- Critical for regulatory adherence
- Alerts when score drops below 80%

**Maintenance Frequency**
- Tracks daily maintenance task volume
- Helps optimize resource allocation
- Identifies maintenance patterns

**Risk Level Assessment**
- Analyzes overdue high-priority tasks
- Provides risk level indicators (Low/Medium/High)
- Enables proactive risk management

### 🔮 Predictive Maintenance
**Pattern Analysis**
- Analyzes historical maintenance data
- Identifies recurring maintenance cycles
- Calculates optimal scheduling intervals

**Smart Predictions**
- Suggests upcoming maintenance tasks
- Provides confidence levels (60-95%)
- Based on previous maintenance patterns

**Automated Scheduling**
- One-click task creation from predictions
- Maintains maintenance schedules automatically
- Prevents equipment failures

### 🚨 Compliance Alerts
**Overdue Detection**
- Identifies overdue compliance tasks
- Severity levels: Low (1-3 days), Medium (4-7 days), High (>7 days)
- Real-time alert generation

**Deadline Monitoring**
- Tracks upcoming compliance deadlines
- Proactive notifications (7-day advance warning)
- Prevents compliance violations

**Risk Assessment**
- Evaluates compliance risk levels
- Provides actionable recommendations
- Maintains regulatory compliance

### 💡 Auto-Suggestions
**Recurring Task Patterns**
- Analyzes completed task patterns
- Suggests optimal scheduling intervals
- Learns from your workflow

**Workload Optimization**
- Monitors team member workload distribution
- Suggests task reassignments
- Balances team productivity

**Smart Recommendations**
- Context-aware task suggestions
- Confidence-based recommendations
- One-click implementation

## How to Use AI Analytics

### 1. Enable AI Analytics
1. Open MainPro Calendar
2. Click **⚙️ Settings**
3. Scroll to **🤖 AI Analytics & Auto-Suggestions**
4. Configure desired features:
   - ✅ Predictive Maintenance
   - ✅ Compliance Alerts
   - ✅ Auto-Suggestions
5. Click **🤖 Enable AI Analytics**

### 2. View Analytics Dashboard
1. Click **🤖 AI** button in toolbar
2. Review performance metrics
3. Check AI insights and recommendations
4. Apply suggested tasks and optimizations

### 3. Manage Predictive Tasks
1. Open AI Analytics Dashboard
2. Review **🔮 Predictive Maintenance** section
3. Click **Add Task** to implement suggestions
4. Monitor confidence levels and reasoning

### 4. Respond to Compliance Alerts
1. Check **🚨 Compliance Alerts** section
2. Review alert severity and descriptions
3. Take immediate action on high-priority alerts
4. Address overdue compliance tasks

### 5. Apply Auto-Suggestions
1. Review **💡 Auto Suggestions** section
2. Click **Apply** to implement suggestions
3. Click **Dismiss** to ignore recommendations
4. Monitor suggestion effectiveness

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**❌ "No AI Insights Yet"**
- Ensure AI Analytics is enabled
- Add more tasks to generate patterns
- Check data quality and completeness

**❌ "Predictions Not Accurate"**
- Verify historical data quality
- Ensure sufficient data points (minimum 3 tasks)
- Review and adjust prediction parameters

**❌ "Alerts Not Showing"**
- Check compliance task categorization
- Verify alert settings and thresholds
- Ensure tasks have proper due dates
